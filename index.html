Fetch Tech

Fetch partners promising early-mid stage startups and provides them with the space, community, legal and operation management to help them start or extend their tech teams in Vietnam.

Our Philosophy

Fetch provides the community, education, connections, and mentorship to help anyone start or grow their tech team through us in Vietnam.

We partner with local tech universities and also have our own academy to groom developers to provide holistic, long-term supply of tech talents for startups throughout their journey. Above all, we believe in humanizing the tech recruitment process, encouraging inclusivity and diversity, and connecting people to one another.

Fetch is a paid-partner and will partner with you for as long as you need. We do not take equity in member companies, and pricing is uniform and transparent. Fetch fits a wide variety of early stages, including pre-accelerator and postaccelerator. Weʼre your long-term partner and friend.
